Who are the Akan?

The Akan is a unique group of people found in Ghana and Ivory Coast. The Akan language is known as Fante or Twi. Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born and depending on their gender. Some prominent people from the Akan community include Kofi Annan, John Atta Mills, and Kwame Nkrumah.
